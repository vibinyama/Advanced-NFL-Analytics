dppg+ is an attempt at calculating how good a defense was in a given season, accounting for teams played, era, and general simplicity.

I chose not to normalize the stat at 100, as I believe being able to see the real number is valuable.

dppg+ can be interpreted as such: The average difference in points that a given team allowed to their opponents, as compared to their opponents average points scored, over the course of the season.
If a team has a +6 dppg+, it means that on average that team allowed 6 LESS points to their opponents than they normally scored. 

The dppg+ formula is as follows: 
dppg+ = ((opponent total points - opponent points to given team)/games played) - opponent points to given team

Using that formula for every game, you then divide by games played to get the dppg+ total over the course of a season.
